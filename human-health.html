HUMAN HEALTH

We have evolved into an innovative global life science company that explores, develops, and markets a broad range of HUMAN HEALTH products. The medicines offered by us are widely appreciated for their correct formulation, accurate composition, longer shelf life, effectiveness and non-toxic nature. With an aim to ensure the presence of afore-named features, the formulations we offer to our valued clients are manufactured in sync with the GMP, WHO, and ISO 9001:2000 standards. The input materials are sourced from reliable agents. Further, we pass these medicines under a quality check so as to ensure their compliance with the industry set quality standards and norms. Due to our quality medicines, we have attained a huge clients from all across the globe.

We supply all kind of dosage form, including Tablets, Capsules, Dry Syrups, Liquid Orals, External Preparations, SVPs (Injectables), Ointments, Powders, etc.
